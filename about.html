ABOUT TAFES

In 1973 the national movement in Uganda (FOCUS Uganda) was registered.

Before its registration, the national movement in Tanzania (FOCUS Tanzania) operated under the Scripture Union of Tanzania until 1990 when it was registered as Tanzania Fellowship of Evangelical Students (TAFES).

To date, TAFES is still affiliated to the International Fellowship of Evangelical Students (IFES) together with other student movements in more than 170 countries worldwide.

TAFES Headquarters is located at Plot 725 Boko, Off Ununio Road Dar es Salaam at our Student Center, in the United Republic of Tanzania.

Mission

To Equip University and College students with the Scripture in Evangelism, Discipleship and Leadership Development thereby transforming the Campus, Church and the Society.

Let’s educate children for a good lifestyle with us. Our donation is hope.

Vision

To be a national movement reaching every student in all Universities and Colleges in Tanzania with the gospel of Jesus Christ.

The Doctrinal Basis of Faith

TAFES has a doctrinal basis of faith which is the fundamental truth of Christianity:

  • The unity of the Father, Son and Holy Spirit in the Godhead.
  • The sovereignty of God in creation, revelation, redemption and final judgment.
  • The divine inspiration and trustworthiness of the Holy Scriptures and its supreme authority.
  • The universal sinfulness and guilt of all men since the fall.
  • Redemption solely through the sacrificial death of the Lord Jesus Christ.
  • The bodily resurrection and ascension of Jesus Christ.
  • The presence and power of the Holy Spirit in regeneration.
  • Justification by grace through faith alone.
  • The indwelling and work of the Holy Spirit in the believer.
  • The one Holy Universal Church, the Body of Christ.
  • The expectation of the personal return of Jesus Christ.
  • The unity of Believers in Christ.

Our Core Commitment

  • Leadership Development: Building tomorrow’s leaders today through student leadership growth.
  • Discipleship: Deepening students’ spiritual life and equipping them for lifetime service.
  • Evangelism: Prioritizing campus outreach to win students for Christ.
  • Missions: Empowering students to take the gospel beyond campuses to rural and global areas.
  • Prayer: Winning campuses and nations through personal and intercessory prayers.
  • Scripture Engagement: In-depth Bible study and faithful interpretation of Scripture.
  • Service to Church & Community: Supporting churches and local communities through student development and outreach.

Our Core Values

  • Evangelical: Bible is our final authority in all faith and conduct matters.
  • Interdenominational & Nondenominational: Open to all believers, serving across all church backgrounds without aligning to a single denomination.
  • Student-Centred: Focused on students and promoting biblical leadership and initiatives.
  • Unity of Believers: Providing a platform for all believers to interact as one Body in Christ.
  • Excellence and Integrity: Promoting godly character and academic excellence with Christ as our role model.
